Chamunda Devi Temple

Chamunda Devi is regarded as a mother goddess in Himachal and thus receives high regard in the state.
According to legend, around 400 hundred years ago, a priest and the King of Kangra prayed to the goddess to let them shift the temple to a more accessible location, which the goddess agreed to.
The Goddess appeared in the dream of the king and told him where to relocate the temple.
