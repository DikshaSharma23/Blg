Mittha

Mittha as the name suggests is a local dessert of Himachal Pradesh.
It is prepared with sweetened rice mixed with a generous helping of raisins and various other dry fruits.

Sidu is local bread made from wheat flour.
This dough is put on direct flame of bonfire to be par-cooked and then later steamed to complete the cooking.


Babru

Babru is like a Himachali variation of the popular kachoris.
It is made by stuffing soaked and ground black gram daal paste to worked dough patties.
These patties are after that rolled and deep fried and offered with tamarind chutney.



Kullu Trout

This fish delicacy made with marinated trout superficial fried in mustard oil with spice is a much loved recipe of the Kullu region.
Cooked in minimal spices, the dish brings out the natural flavours of the delicious trout.
